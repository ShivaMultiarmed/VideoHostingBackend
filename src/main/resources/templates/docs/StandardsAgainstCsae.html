Trendy App is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all users, including minors. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward content or behavior that promotes, depicts, or facilitates child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on Trendy App:

  • Images, videos, text, or other media depicting or describing the sexual abuse or exploitation of children.
  • Content that promotes, glorifies, or normalizes sexual relationships between adults and minors.
  • Drawings, cartoons, or digitally generated content that depict CSAE.
  • Links to external sites or platforms that contain or promote CSAE content.

2. Prohibited behavior

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited on Trendy App:

  • Attempting to contact minors for sexual purposes (grooming).
  • Soliciting or distributing CSAE material.
  • Using Trendy App features to exploit or endanger minors.

3. Reporting and Enforcement

  • We provide users with a clear and accessible method to report suspected CSAE content or behavior.
  • Reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.
  • Confirmed violations will result in permanent suspension of the offending account and removal of illegal content.
  • We report CSAE content and offenders to the appropriate law enforcement agencies and organizations.

4. Preventative Measures

  • We use human moderation to detect and block CSAE content.
  • We educate users about safe online behavior and reporting mechanisms.

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Trendy App fully cooperates with law enforcement and child protection agencies in investigating and prosecuting CSAE offenses.
